CharGen

Fantasy Character Generator

Summon your new champion

Quickly enter your tabletop realm of adventure with this app to create randomized character profiles. Auto-generate, edit, store and share your characters all in one place.

Randomize your fate

Or don't. The character creation form can be auto-completed with a push of a button. If you disagree with fate, overwrite the input fields and set up your characters the old fashioned way.

Expand your party

Keep an archive of your past character sheets. Search by name. Sort by class, race, name or level.

Level up!

Characters develop over time. As you adventure, update your items, experience, spells, abilities and more. As you modify your stats, the rest of your sheet will recalculate.

Roll the dice

Roll any combination of virtual dice to determine how your next encounter will proceed.
